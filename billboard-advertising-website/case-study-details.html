EV Brand Launch Expressway Domination Campaign

The Challenge

A premier Electric Vehicle manufacturer wanted to build rapid regional awareness for its brand launch. The goal was to target high-income commuters traveling into central business districts without wasting ad spend on offline print media that could not be tracked.

The Strategy

We locked down 5 premium digital LED bulletins positioned at key highway interchanges where vehicle congestion peaks during commuter hours. Using weather triggers, when regional temperatures exceeded 80Â°F, creatives automatically adjusted to promote cabin climate controls, driving a 34% increase in nearby dealership foot traffic.

Execution Outcomes

By integrating mobile device location logs, we mapped vehicle movement against the billboard coordinates, verifying that over 8.4 million impressions were generated over the 4-week flight. Dealership visits inside the LA target circle surged by 34% compared to standard control periods.
