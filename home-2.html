FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have queries about OOH placements, production schedules, or pricing? Find rapid answers below.

What is the difference between digital and traditional OOH?
Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) utilizes high-impact LED/LCD screens allowing dynamic programmatic content changes, weather/dayparting triggers, and multiple creative schedules. Traditional OOH utilizes physical printed vinyl layouts wrapped onto roadside frames, securing exclusive round-the-clock display ownership.
How do you calculate daily audience impressions?
Audience metrics are derived from mobile location logs, traffic census audits, and local commuter route databases. This ensures reliable data tracking to calculate your estimated views, CPM rates, and unique demographic reach indices.
Can I customize the active run times of my digital ad spots?
Yes, through our advertiser dashboard, you can schedule slots matching specific rush hours, dayparts, or meteorological triggers (like temperature or rainfall alerts).
What are the lead times for vinyl printing and installation?
Traditional physical campaigns require printed vinyl shipments. Average production and installation lead times are 10–14 business days from approved creative submission. Digital board campaigns can be uploaded and activated within 2 hours.
