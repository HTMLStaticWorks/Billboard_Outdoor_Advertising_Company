Digital Billboard Display Networks

Vibrant colors, remote creative uploads, dayparting, and context-aware triggers for maximum conversion.

Why Programmatic Digital OOH?

Unlike traditional paper bulletins, digital out-of-home (DOOH) allows brands to dynamically swap visual copy in real-time, customize flights according to hour parameters, and purchase advertising inventory programmatically.

Dayparting Schedules

Serve coffee ads in the morning, food deals in the afternoon, and nightlife promos after dark.

Weather & Event Triggers

Link ad play triggers to temperature drops, local sports scores, or retail inventory levels.

Hardware Specs

Built for Crisp Outdoor Visibility

Our displays feature high-density LED modules ensuring readability even under direct mid-day sunlight.

8,500 Nits Brightness

Auto-dimming sensors adjust illumination to maintain visual comfort day and night.

Pixel Pitch (10mm)

Ultra-fine spacing delivers high-fidelity artwork to pedestrians and traffic streams.

LTE Cloud Upload

Launch creative copy changes in less than 5 minutes globally from the control board.
